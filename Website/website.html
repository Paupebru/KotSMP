Server Rules

1. General Conduct (Chat & Interaction) ✉️ 💬

👉 - Respectful conduct: No insults, bullying, or provocations in the chat or on Discord.😡
👉 - No spam: No spamming messages or excessive use of all-caps.✉️
👉 - No unauthorized external advertising: No promotion of other Minecraft servers, YouTube channels, or Discord links without permission.🛑
👉 - Appropriate skins & names: No offensive or insulting skins or player names.🦺

2. Gameplay & Fairness🕹️ ⛔

👉- Strict ban on cheating: Mods, hacks, or texture packs that provide unfair advantages (e.g., X-Ray, fly hacks, KillAura) are strictly prohibited.✖️
👉- No griefing: Destroying or altering other players' structures without their permission is prohibited.😱
👉- No stealing: Items must not be stolen from other players' chests or furnaces.🤥
👉- Bug exploitation is prohibited: Exploiting game errors (bugs) or duplication glitches is forbidden. Errors must be reported to the team.🤓

3. Building rules & server performance👾 🏠

👉- Maintain distance: When building, keep a sufficient distance from your neighbors' plots (e.g., at least 50–100 blocks), unless agreed otherwise.🔛
👉- Lag prevention (Redstone & entities): * Continuously running Redstone circuits (clocks) must be capable of being turned off and must not run while you are offline.⏰

4. PvP (Player vs. Player) & Raiding⚔️

👉- For peaceful servers (PvE): PvP is permitted only in designated arenas or by mutual agreement. Player traps (lethal traps) are prohibited.📌
👉- For PvP/Factions servers: PvP is permitted everywhere. However, camping at spawn points (spawn-killing) or dropping lava or water onto enemy bases may be restricted.🎮
